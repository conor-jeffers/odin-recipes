New Sibling

This one's for the kiddies, who only wanted a little brother or sister to play with while mum and dad were busy.

Ingredients

  • A pin, or something else that's small and sharp.
  • One of dad's weird, sticky balloon things.

Steps

  1. The first thing you'l need is access to mum and dad's room, and a bit of patience.
  2. When they're busy doing something else, go into their room and look for the little box of wrappers that dad keeps his balloons in.
  3. Very carefully poke the pin into the pack, without leaving toomuch of an obvious mark.
  4. Now, just wait a few weeks until mum starts crying - with joy, of course!

This one really depends on not being caught, so ber sure to make sure no one's around when you do it, and that you don't rip dad's balloons to the point of it being obvious.
