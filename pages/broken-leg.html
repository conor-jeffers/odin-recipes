Broken Leg

Fancy some time off work? Just follow this cartoonish set of instructions to net yourself some well earned time out of the office!

Ingredients

  • A lovely, ripe banana
  • Access to a flight of stairs (preferrably in work)
  • A high pain tolerance

Steps

  1. First off, peel and eat your big, sweaty banana.
  2. Throw it on the ground, right at the top of a big ol' flight of stairs.
  3. Leave the scene briefly, and then reappear as if to head down the stairs, watching your feet as you go.
  4. If you watched your feet well enough, you hsould have been able to land a step on the banana peel, which should have now sent you flying down the flight of stairs.
  5. Wait for someone to call an ambulane, and consider your job done!

This might suck to pull off, but done right, it can easily get you a few weeks off work. Have fun!
